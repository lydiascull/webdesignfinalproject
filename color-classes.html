COLOR CLASSES

What are color classes?

All colors on the color wheel have their own class, which is how they are grouped together. In the context of the color wheen, there are three color classes.

The main six colors on the color wheel are divided into primary and secondary color groups. The colors in between these six are in the tertiary color group.

1. Primary Colors: Primary colors simply exist and cannot be created by mixing other colors.

Red

Yellow

Blue

2. Secondary Colors: Secondary colors are created by mixing two primary colors together.

Orange

Red + Yellow

Green

Yellow + Blue

Purple

Red + Blue

3. Tertiary Colors: Tertiary colors are created by mixing a primary color with a secondary color. Because of this pattern,
there is the same amount of tertiary colors and primary and secondary color combined.

Tertiary colors are named after the two colors that make them.

Red-Orange

Yellow-Orange

Yellow-Green

Blue-Green

Blue-Purple

Red-Purple
